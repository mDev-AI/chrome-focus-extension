How to Add Channels

There are multiple ways to add channels to your whitelist:

  1. Enter the channel name or ID in the input field above
  2. Right-click on a channel link on YouTube and select "Add to Whitelist" from the context menu
  3. Visit a YouTube channel page and click the extension icon to whitelist the current channel
